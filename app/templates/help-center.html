Common causes and solutions:

  • Website requires login: Use public websites or contact us for enterprise solutions
  • URL not accessible: Check if the website is online and accessible
  • Blocked by robots.txt: Some websites block automated access
  • Large website: Training may take longer for sites with extensive content
  • Network issues: Try again in a few minutes

Performance optimization tips:

  • Train your bot on focused, relevant content only
  • Avoid training on entire large websites
  • Use clear, well-structured web pages
  • Limit the training data to essential information
  • Consider upgrading to Pro plan for faster response times

Improving answer quality:

  • Train on high-quality, well-written content
  • Ensure the training data is relevant to expected questions
  • Use websites with clear, factual information
  • Avoid training on opinion-based or ambiguous content
  • Test with various question phrasings during development
